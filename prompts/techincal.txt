5. **Technology Stack & Tooling**

6. **Testing & Quality**

- Conclude with technical lessons, potential improvements, and future considerations

WRITING STYLE:
- Use precise technical terminology
- Assume reader has solid programming knowledge
- Be thorough and comprehensive - don't skip important details
- Include links to relevant documentation, RFCs, or resources when applicable

- Maintain professional engineering tone while being clear and structured

CONTENT PRIORITIES:
- Prioritize depth over breadth
- Focus on HOW things work and WHY they were built that way
- Show the engineering thinking and problem-solving process
